About Riverbend Outdoor Project

A storytelling and education project based in the St. Louis / Metro East area, focused on making outdoor adventure more approachable for everyday people.

Hi, I’m Justin. I’ve spent years exploring the Midwest on foot and by paddle— from local trails around Edwardsville to multi-day ultras and long-distance river trips.

Riverbend Outdoor Project is my way of sharing stories, routes, and lessons learned so that more people feel confident taking on their own adventures, no matter where they’re starting from.

Whether you’re eyeing your first 50K, planning a backpacking trip, or just looking for inspiration to get outside a bit more often, you’re in the right place.
